Write a patient education handout explaining a complex medical condition (e.g., attention deficit hyperactivity disorder (ADHD)) in simple, compassionate language. Use analogies, diagrams described in text, and FAQs. Include tips for managing the condition at home and when to seek help. The handout should be suitable for a 8th‑grade reading level.
