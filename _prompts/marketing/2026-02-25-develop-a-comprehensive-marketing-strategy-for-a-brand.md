Develop a comprehensive marketing strategy for a brand new product: a pair of glasses that translate sign language into text in real time. The product will be launched in a market dominated by a traditional company slow to change. Your strategy should include: (1) a detailed customer persona for the target audience college students struggling with mental health, (2) a unique selling proposition that leverages creates a sense of belonging through shared experiences, (3) a multi‑channel campaign outline using at least three unconventional channels (e.g., interactive street art with QR codes), (4) a budget allocation table, and (5) a set of key performance indicators to measure success. Write the strategy in the form of a memo to the CEO, with clear sections and persuasive language.
