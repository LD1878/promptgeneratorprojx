Create a negotiation strategy for acquiring a smaller competitor, a traditional company slow to change. Outline your goals, BATNA, key concessions you're willing to make, and tactics. Also prepare a dossier on the competitor: their strengths, weaknesses, likely motivations, and who the decision‑makers are. Finally, write a script for the first meeting.
