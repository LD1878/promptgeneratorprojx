Create a data analysis plan for a study using a mixed‑methods approach. The quantitative part involves a survey (N=500) on the psychology of conspiracy theories, and the qualitative part involves 20 semi‑structured interviews. Describe how you'll analyze the quantitative data (descriptive stats, inferential tests) and qualitative data (thematic analysis), and how you'll integrate findings.
