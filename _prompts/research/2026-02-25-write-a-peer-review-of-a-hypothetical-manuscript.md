Write a peer review of a hypothetical manuscript titled 'Remote Work and Productivity: A Longitudinal Study'. The review should include: (1) a summary of the paper, (2) overall assessment, (3) major comments (e.g., issues with methodology, missing controls), (4) minor comments (e.g., clarity, citations), and (5) a recommendation (accept, minor revisions, major revisions, reject). Be constructive and specific.
