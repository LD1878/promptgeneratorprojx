Write a post‑mortem for a major system outage. The outage affected a video streaming for 6 hours. Describe the timeline, root cause, impact, how it was detected, steps taken to mitigate, and lessons learned. Then propose specific improvements to prevent recurrence, including monitoring, testing, and architectural changes.
