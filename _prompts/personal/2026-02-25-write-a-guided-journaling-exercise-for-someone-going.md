Write a guided journaling exercise for someone going through a major life transition (e.g., starting a new job). The exercise should include: (1) a centering meditation, (2) prompts to explore feelings of loss and gain, (3) questions about what they're learning, (4) a visualization of their future self, and (5) an action step for the week. Write in a warm, encouraging tone.
